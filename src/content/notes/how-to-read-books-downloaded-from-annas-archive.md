Select from the slow downloads options, unless you're a member.

I can't figure out how to add these books to my Kindle (see [[notes/read-epub-books-on-kindle|Read EPUB Books on Kindle]], but you can open them with [Calibre](https://calibre-ebook.com/) and read them there, or convert them to EPUB and read them in [Readwise](https://readwise.io/).

To upload to Readwise, select the book and click **Save to Disk.** Then go to Readwise and upload from wherever you saved it.

If the site won't load, try [these mirrors](https://shadowlibraries.github.io/DirectDownloads/AnnasArchive/).
