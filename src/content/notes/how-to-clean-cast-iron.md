title: How to Clean Cast Iron
private: false
created: 2026-07-23
updated: 2026-07-23
---

1. Rinse under hot water and scrub[^1]
2. Wipe on a thin layer of canola oil
3. Heat over medium
4. When the oil begins to smoke lightly, reduce heat and keep it there 5–10 minutes

[^1]: Nothing too abrasive. Minimal or no soap.
