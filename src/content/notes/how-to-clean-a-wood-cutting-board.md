1. Sprinkle the board with coarse salt and squeeze a lemon over it. Use the lemon like a sponge to scrub the board.[^1] Wipe down with a clean dry cloth, and let the board dry completely.
2. Spray the board with vinegar and wipe it with a clean dry cloth. Let the board dry completely.
3. Treat the board with food safe mineral oil.
4. Treat the board with butcher block conditioner.

[^1]: I can't actually tell if this does anything, but I've only tried it once.
