If you're subscribed to market data in IBKR, and have connected IBRK to TradingView, you don't need to pay for the data in Trading again. See [I have a subscription to market data from Interactive Brokers, can I use this data on the TradingView?](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000669300/).

Caveat: On TradingView, you can only be logged into IBKR in one tab at a time, so you'll only get your IBKR data in that one tab.
