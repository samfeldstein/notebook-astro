Too much is decided by dice. Undercuts collective storytelling agency. May as well have a computer calling the shots.

Level system because it explicitly makes some characters/creatures better than others. Odds of victory should never be zero, but with level system, they often are. A peasant should be able to kill an arch wizard if the players are clever enough and the story wants things to go that way.
