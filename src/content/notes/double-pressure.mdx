When bulls and bears temporarily join forces on the same side of the market.

<BlockQuote work="Understanding Price Action Trading" author="Bob Volman" medium="book">
For prices to follow through in substantial fashion, we need assistance from both sides of the market. Should we aim to take position on, say, the bull side, it may not sufÏce to only find companions in the bullish camp. Preferably, we would like to see a decent number of bears quickly bail out to protect themselves from the very rising market we are trying to exploit. The more bears forced to buy back their shorts, the better the odds for our trade to reach its destination before the situation sees chance to reverse.
</BlockQuote>
