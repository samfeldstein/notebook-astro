1. Dampen a microfiber cloth with warm water and wring it out until it is nearly dry.
2. Add one drop of mild dish soap to the cloth.
3. Wipe gently with the direction of the wood grain.
4. Immediately follow with a fresh, dry towel to absorb any remaining moisture.
