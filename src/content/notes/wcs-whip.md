To keep torque off your knee, open towards your follow on **three and**. Set your hip back (back, not out; don't pop it) to wind the pivot. Release your hip and let the pivot happen on your left foot. A half turn will do it. So you're perpendicular to the slot on **five.**

Strike with toe, but pivot on heel. More stable.
