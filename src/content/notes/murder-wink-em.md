Everyone sits in a circle and draws a piece of paper from a bucket. Most are blank, but one will have an M or something to identify the "murderer." A person is killed when the murderer makes eye contact and winks. The more dramatic and drawn out the death the better. The murder tries to "kill" as many people as they can without anyone seeing who they are and identifying them.
