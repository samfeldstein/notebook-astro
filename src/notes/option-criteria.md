- Liquid only: gauge with slippage
	- Lower slippage = higher liquidity
	- Never trade options with slippage over 2% of the stock price
- Low IV
	- Premium increases with IV, so if you buy at low IV, more chance of accruing value as option demand rises, and IV with it
