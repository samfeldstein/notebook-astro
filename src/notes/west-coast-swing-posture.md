Pretend your head is a balloon. [[how-to-breathe-in-west-coast-swing|inhale, exhale]]. Exhale. Feel your neck lengthen and your head float toward the ceiling. Pull the top your skull straight backward; feel the muscles on the back of your neck engage.

Feel your shoulders drop as your lats engage. Activate your glutes and thighs. Feel your tailbone pull fall toward the floor.
