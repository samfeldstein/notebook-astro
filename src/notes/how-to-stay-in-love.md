- Check in every morning
	- Ask what's on their mind, what they're looking forward to, what they're worried about
	- Ask if there's anything they need from you today
- Ask questions
	- [Gottman Card Decks](https://www.gottman.com/couples/apps/)
- Write down why you fell in love and read it every year on your anniversary
- Write love letters to each other
- Have annual reviews
