Ultimately the right choice is the one that will be the most comfortable.

- Noise
	- Wall thickness
	- Wind
	- Dogs (neighbors?)
- Appliances
- AC/Heat
- Utilities
- Wifi
- Laundry
- Parking
- Move-in date
- Lease length
- Take video

## Other things to consider

- Landlord/management company reputation
