## Level One
 
- Would you like to be famous?
- What would you like to be famous for?
- Have you ever sang to anyone?
- Where were you a year ago?
- Where were you five years ago?
- Where were you ten years ago?
- If you could live anywhere, where would you live?
- What's something you're good at?
- What's something you're bad at?
- Have you ever felt genuine affection for an inanimate object?
- Who's your best friend?
- Why is your best friend your best friend?
- What's something you've done that you're proud of?
- Who do you admire?
- What's something you recently learned about yourself?

## Level Two

- How have you changed in the last year?
- How have you changed in the last five years?
- How have you changed in the last ten years?
- What hasn't changed about you since you were a kid?
- What do you like about youself?
- What don’t you like about yourself?
- What would you change about the way you were raised?
- What drives you?
- What makes you anxious?
- What do you do when you get anxious?
- What's one mistake you'll never make?
- What are you grateful for?

## Level Three

- What do you do when you need to cry?
- Who do you talk to when you need someone to talk to?
- When’s the last time you cried?
- When's the last time you cried in front of someone?
- Have you ever broken someone’s heart?
- Have you ever had your heart broken?
- What's your most embarassing memory?
- What's a memory you treasure?
- What's the worst thing you've ever done?
