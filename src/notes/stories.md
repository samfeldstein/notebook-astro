Storytelling is the art of emotional manipulation. Emotions are levers. Storytellers know how to pull them, which to pull, and when.

Any given beat elicits an emotion.

## What are stories for

Stories should be motivating forces. If they aren't, they're just entertainment, and probably a waste of time.
