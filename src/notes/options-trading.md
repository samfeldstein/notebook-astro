## How to finds stocks

- Read the news
- Watch for buybacks
- Watch for earnings releases

### Earnings reports to watch for

- NVDA
- WMT
- YELP

## Option criteria

- Liquid only: gauge with bid/ask
	- Tighter spread = higher liquidity
	- The difference between the bid and ask prices is sometimes called “slippage.”
	- Never trade options with slippage over 2% of the stock price
