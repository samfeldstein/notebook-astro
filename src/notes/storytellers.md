Storytelling is the art of emotional manipulation. Emotions are levers. Storytellers know how to pull them, which ones to pull, and when.

Any given beat elicits a reaction:

- Yes!
- No!
- Huh?
- Ew!
- Phew!
- What?!

And so on.
