---
title: How to Treat a Cold
tags: ["health"]
updated: 2025-01-06
---

For congestion:

- Oxymetazoline nasal spray from the dollar store.
- Drink lots of water.

For a sore throat:

- Gargle salt water (1/2 t salt, 4 oz warm water).
- Drink Peppermint tea with honey.

For everything:

- Take acetaminophen.
